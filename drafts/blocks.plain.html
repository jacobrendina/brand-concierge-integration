Accordion FAQs

What is the minimum order?

There's no hard minimum. Order a single pair to sample, or scale to tens of thousands for a full merch drop.

How long does production take?

Most orders ship within two weeks of design approval. Rush production is available for time-sensitive launches.

Can you match my exact brand colours?

Yes. We carry over 200 yarn colours and offer custom Pantone dyeing for an exact match.
